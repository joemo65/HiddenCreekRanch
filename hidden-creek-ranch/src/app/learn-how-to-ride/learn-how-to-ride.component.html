Learn How to Ride

NO HORSE REQUIRED!

As part of our goal for a safe environment, we have horses that will provide a secure learning experience to our riders. Our horses are able to work with novice riders, as well as advanced level riders. They range in size to fit each rider, from the small child to the large adult.

BE PART OF A SHOW TEAM

AQHA – breed circuit. Qualifies for AQHA Novice World Show.

USHJA Affiliate - Hunter/Jumper circuit. Qualifies for OCHSA or IEHJA year end show.

Local – Highlander Open Horse Shows in La Habra. (English or Western with year end high point awards.)

Barn Duty

  • Riding horses is not the only part of keeping a horse
  • Barn duty days are included in your riding lesson
  • Do not need to own or lease your own horse to participate
  • Learn how to clean a stall, feed, wash and oil saddles
  • Learn how to pack a trailer for a show
  • Learn to handle a horse at hand

Merit Badge Workshops

  • Boy or Girl scout groups are welcomed
  • Workshops to fit your group's needs
  • Cost: $25 per person
  • Scheduled for the 1st or 3rd Saturdays from 2 to 4 pm
  • Workshop includes: Grooming, safety and a mini lesson on horseback
